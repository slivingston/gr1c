Verify well-formedness (as much as gr1c demands) of GR(1) specification. Whether the initial conditions (i.e., ENVINIT and SYSINIT) are in acceptable form depends on init_flags, which corresponds to the argument by the same name used with check_realizable() and synthesize() (cf. solve.h). Use UNDEFINED_INIT in init_flags to avoid checking particular interpretations of initial conditions.

Expand any variables with integral domains in parse trees of the GR(1) specification components.

init_flags as used with check_realizable() and synthesize() (cf. solve.h) is necessary to decide how restrictions of unreachable values that result from nonbool expansion should be incorporated into initial conditions. The _init trees are assumed to be well-formed given init_flags, as verified by check_gr1c_form().

Parameters
verboselevel of detail in logging; larger implies more detail. 0 (zero) to be quiet.
